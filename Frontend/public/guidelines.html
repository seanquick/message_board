Community Guidelines

Last updated: Aug 10, 2025

Welcome! To maintain a respectful and productive community, we ask that you follow these rules:

Quick Navigation
  • Be civil
  • No hate or harassment
  • No illegal or dangerous content
  • Respect privacy
  • Stay on topic & add value
  • No spam or self‑promotion abuse
  • No NSFW
  • Enforcement
  • Reporting & appeals

Be civil

Assume good intent. Disagree with ideas, not people. No name‑calling, mockery, or insults.

No hate or harassment

Do not target individuals or groups based on protected characteristics. No threats, bullying, or dog‑piling.

No illegal or dangerous content

No facilitation of illegal activity, violence, or harm. Do not share malware or instructions to cause damage.

Respect privacy

No doxxing or posting private information (addresses, phone numbers, private messages) without consent.

Stay on topic & add value

Share clear titles, useful details, and sources where possible. Low‑effort or off‑topic posts may be removed.

No spam or self‑promotion abuse

Relevant links are okay; excessive promotions, referral links, or repetitive content are not.

No NSFW

Sexual, pornographic, or extremely graphic content is not allowed.

Enforcement

Moderators may remove content, limit features, or suspend/ban accounts. Admins can see author identities for moderation even when posts are anonymous.

Reporting & appeals

Use the “Report” button on posts or comments to flag issues. For account actions you believe are in error, contact an admin.
